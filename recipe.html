Chicken Biryani

Fragrant and spicy layered rice and chicken dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken (marinated)
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Biryani spices (garam masala, turmeric, cumin, etc.)
  • Saffron & Milk (for color)
  • Fresh coriander and mint

Instructions:

1. Cook basmati rice 70% through and set aside.
2. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
3. Cook marinated chicken with spices and yogurt until tender.
4. Layer chicken and rice in a pot. Add fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
5. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
6. Serve hot with raita.
